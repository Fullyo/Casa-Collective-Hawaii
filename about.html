About Us

Meet the family behind Casa Collective Hawaii

Our Story

Hello, I'm Meg, a Michigan native with a passion for creating unique and memorable experiences. With a background in interior design, property management, and personal concierge services, I've brought a distinctive touch to our short-term rental that sets it apart from the rest on the island.

When you step into our space, you'll immediately notice a decor that goes beyond the ordinary. I believe in offering more than just a place to rest your head – I want you to truly enjoy every aspect of your visit, engaging your mind, body, and soul. Our properties feature dedicated meditation spaces on private lanais, providing a tranquil retreat for reflection and relaxation.

My connection to the Hawaiian Islands goes beyond the physical—it's a calling I've felt since my childhood. My husband, a proud Kailua Kona local, and I are raising our two beautiful children on this gorgeous island. By choosing our rentals, you're gaining the unique perspective of a local homegrown boy and a Michigan import.

We are dedicated to crafting the best accommodations for our guests, and it brings us immense joy to share with you the rich culture, natural beauty, and aloha spirit that defines the Big Island.

Mahalo,
Meg
