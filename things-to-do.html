Things To Do

Adventures & Tours on the Big Island

Our Island Favorites

One of our greatest joys is sharing the magic of the Big Island with our guests. These are a few of our family's favorite spots to explore, relax, and connect with the spirit of aloha. We hope you love them as much as we do.

Coffee Tours

  • Kona Coffee & Tea: A family-owned, single-estate farm in the heart of Kona offering tours from seed to cup. Book a tour
  • Greenwell Farms: One of Kona's oldest and most famous coffee farms.
  • Mountain Thunder: Experience a unique cloud forest coffee plantation.

Beaches

  • Kealakekua Bay: A historic bay perfect for snorkeling and seeing dolphins.
  • Manini Beach Park: A small, secluded beach with calm waters.
  • Ho'okena Beach Park: A local favorite with beautiful gray sand.

Snorkeling

  • Captain Cook Monument: Some of the best snorkeling in Hawaii, accessible by boat or a challenging hike.
  • Two Step: Easy ocean access from lava rock, teeming with vibrant fish.
  • Kahalu'u Beach Park: A great spot for beginners and families to see sea turtles.

Hikes

  • Captain Cook Monument Trail: A steep but rewarding hike down to the bay.
  • Pu'uhonua o Hōnaunau Park: Explore a historic place of refuge with beautiful coastal trails.
  • Makaula O‘oma Trail: Also known as the Cloud Forest, this lush, shaded hike in Kona’s uplands leads you through native Hawaiian forest.
